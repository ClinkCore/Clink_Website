Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 5, 2025

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

Introduction

Clink Innovated, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Clink mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information You Provide

When you register for and use our App, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password, first name, last name
  • Profile Information: Date of birth, gender, hometown, sexual orientation, relationship status, bio, interests, favorite drink, academic major, academic status
  • University Information: School/university affiliation (verified through university email)
  • Profile Media: Profile pictures and photos (up to 6 photos)
  • Moments: Temporary photos and videos you share (automatically expire after 7 days)
  • Chat Communications: Messages, images, and audio recordings you send through our messaging feature
  • Business Information (for bar/business accounts): Business name, business type, operating hours, phone number, website, business description, amenities, price range, business license number

2. Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Information: Online status, last active timestamp, typing indicators in conversations
  • Social Interactions: Friend lists, friend requests, likes, matches, blocked users, moment views and likes, comment interactions
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens for sending notifications
  • Technical Data: IP addresses (used for security purposes only, not stored permanently)

3. Location Information

  • Bar/Business Location: If you register as a bar or business, we collect and store your business location to help customers find you
  • Location Services: We only request location access for business accounts. We do not track or collect location data from regular user accounts

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

To be clear about our privacy practices, we do NOT collect:

  • Financial Information or credit card data
  • Health & Fitness Data
  • Browsing History outside our app
  • Cross-App Tracking Data
  • Diagnostic Data or crash logs
  • Precise Location from Regular Users (only business accounts)

How We Use Your Information

In accordance with Apple's App Store requirements, we clearly describe how we use each type of data we collect:

Primary Uses (App Functionality)

  • Account Management: Create and manage your account, authenticate your identity
  • Profile Features: Display your profile to other users, enable customization
  • Social Networking: Connect you with friends, facilitate friend requests, enable matches
  • Messaging: Enable real-time chat, deliver messages, show typing indicators
  • Moments: Allow you to share temporary photos/videos, automatically delete expired content
  • Business Discovery: Display business locations and information to help customers find businesses
  • Fraud Prevention: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

Uses We Do NOT Engage In

  • Third-Party Advertising - We do not display ads or share data for advertising
  • Tracking - We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • Data Selling - We do not sell your personal information
  • Marketing to Third Parties - We do not share your data for others' marketing purposes

How We Share Your Information

Information Shared with Other Users

Your privacy settings control much of what other users can see:

  • Public Profile Information: Username, profile pictures, bio, and interests are visible to other users based on your privacy settings
  • Moments: Visible to "Friends Only" or "Public" based on your chosen visibility setting
  • Online Status: Your online/offline status is visible to users you interact with
  • Business Information: All business profile information is publicly visible

Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate our App:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, file storage, cloud functions, and push notifications
  • Apple Services: Face ID/Touch ID authentication, MapKit for location services (business accounts only), Push Notifications

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase security rules to control data access
  • Secure password hashing through Firebase Authentication
  • Secure storage of credentials using iOS Keychain
  • Regular security updates and patches

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Privacy Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View and download your personal information
  • Update Information: Edit your profile information at any time
  • Delete Content: Remove your moments, messages, and profile pictures
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and associated data
  • Block Users: Prevent specific users from contacting you

Tracking and Advertising

We Do NOT Track You

  • No Cross-App Tracking
  • No Data Brokers
  • No Targeted Advertising
  • No Advertising Networks
  • No Third-Party Analytics Services

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.
